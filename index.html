About Me

Welcome to my portfolio site!  I'm Joe Hollenbach, a software engineer based in Northern Indiana.  I am a recent college graduate and thrive on continuous learning and skill development.

Beyond my professional pursuits, I love the outdoors, spending time with my family, and getting into adventures with my two dogs.

I have always had a deep interest in technology as a hobby and in my work, but before 2021, I worked as an inventory acquisitions manager in the Auto Industry.  There, I used web application tools to acquire, price, and manage large vehicle inventories.  Like so many others, I was laid off due to the 2020 Covid-19 Pandemic; so I decided to return to college and change careers to Software Engineering.

Since that time, I have worked dilligently to learn new skills in software development.  I look forward to pursuing my new career as a software engineer, and connecting with fellow professionals and enthusiasts who share my passion for technology.

Experience

I have earned an Associate of Applied Science degree in Software Development from Ivy Tech Community College.   Through my courses I also acquired numerous certifications, such as a Software Application Developer Certificate and a Technical Certificate in Software Development from Ivy Tech Community College, CompTIA A+ Certification, Amazon Web Services (AWS) Certified Cloud Practitioner, and many others.

I have worked on many software and web application projects throughout my college courses, as well as my own personal projects.  I also work as an Expert at Apple, where I provided customers with face-to-face technical support and sales for iPhones, iPads, Macs, and other Apple devices.
