About Me

Welcome to my profile! I'm Joe Hollenbach, a software engineer based in Northern Indiana. I am a recent college graduate and thrive on continuous learning and skill development.

Beyond my professional pursuits, I love the outdoors, spending time with my family, and getting into adventures with my two dogs.

I'm excited to pursue a career in software engineering and look forward to connecting with fellow professionals and enthusiasts who share my passion for technology.

I have always had a passion for technology, but before the 2020 COVID-19 Pandemic, I worked as an Inventory Manager in the Auto Industry. After suffering a layoff during that period, I decided to return to college and change careers to Software Engineering.

Education & Experience

I have earned an Associate of Applied Science in Software Development from Ivy Tech Community College. I have also earned numerous certifications, such as a Software Application Developer Certificate and a Technical Certificate in Software Development from Ivy Tech Community College, CompTIA A+ Certification, Amazon Web Services (AWS) Certified Cloud Practitioner, and many others.

I developed many software and web application development projects throughout my college courses, as well as personal projects. I have also worked as a Technical Specialist at Apple, Inc., where I provided customers with face-to-face technical support for their Apple products.
